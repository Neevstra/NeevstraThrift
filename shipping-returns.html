Shipping & Returns

Information about our shipping policies, delivery times, and return process

Shipping Information

At Neevstra, we strive to deliver your sustainable fashion finds to you as quickly and efficiently as possible. We ship to most countries worldwide and offer various shipping options to meet your needs.

Shipping Methods & Delivery Times

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days €9.99
Express Shipping 2-3 business days €14.99
Next Day Delivery 1 business day (order before 2 PM) €19.99
International Shipping 7-14 business days €24.99

Free Shipping: Orders over ₹999 qualify for free standard shipping across India.

Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation SMS and email with tracking information.

Shipping Coverage

We deliver to all major cities and towns across India including Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Pune, and many more. Some remote locations in hilly areas or islands may require additional delivery time of 1-2 days.

Cash on Delivery

We offer Cash on Delivery (COD) service across India with an additional charge of ₹49. COD orders are subject to verification and may take slightly longer to process.

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy with your order, we offer a simple returns and exchanges process.

Return Policy

  • You may return items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached.
  • Sale items and accessories (jewelry, scarves, etc.) are final sale and cannot be returned unless defective.
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective or we made an error.

How to Return an Item

  1. Log in to your account and go to your order history.
  2. Select the order containing the item(s) you wish to return.
  3. Fill out the return form, indicating the reason for your return.
  4. Print the return label and packing slip.
  5. Pack the item(s) securely in the original packaging if possible.
  6. Attach the return label to the outside of the package.
  7. Drop off the package at your nearest post office or shipping carrier.

Refund Process

Once we receive your return, our team will inspect the item(s) to ensure they meet our return policy requirements. Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days after inspection. The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Exchanges

If you would like to exchange an item for a different size or color, please indicate this on your return form. If the item you want is available, we will ship it to you once we receive your return. If there is a price difference, we will either charge or refund the difference accordingly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change or cancel my order?
You can cancel or modify your order within 2 hours of placing it by contacting our customer service team. After this time, your order may have already been processed for shipping and cannot be modified.
What if my package is lost or damaged?
If your package is lost or arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of the expected delivery date. We will work with the shipping carrier to locate your package or process a replacement/refund.
Do you deliver to all parts of India?
Yes, we deliver to all major cities and towns across India. We cover metro cities, tier-2 cities, and most rural areas. Some remote locations in hilly regions or islands may require additional 1-2 days for delivery. Pin code verification is available at checkout.
How do I track my order?
Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history.
What if the item I want is out of stock?
Since we sell unique vintage and second-hand items, most products are one-of-a-kind and may not be restocked once sold out. You can sign up for notifications to be alerted when similar items become available.
Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available?
Yes, we offer Cash on Delivery across India with an additional charge of ₹49. COD orders are subject to phone verification before dispatch. Please ensure someone is available to receive the package and make payment to the delivery person.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about shipping, returns, or your order, our customer service team is here to help.
