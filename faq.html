Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

We offer two shipping options across India:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days (₹99)
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days (₹199)

Orders above ₹2,999 qualify for free standard shipping. Shipping times may vary during peak seasons or for remote locations.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within India. We're working on expanding our shipping options to international destinations in the future. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when international shipping becomes available.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on our website or directly through the courier's tracking portal. If you haven't received tracking information within 48 hours of your order confirmation, please contact our customer service team.

Are your products authentic vintage items?

Yes, all our products are authentic vintage or pre-loved items. Each piece is carefully selected, cleaned, and when necessary, repaired to ensure it meets our quality standards. We provide detailed information about the era, condition, and any notable characteristics of each item in the product description.

How do you determine sizing for vintage clothing?

Vintage sizing often differs from modern sizing standards. For each clothing item, we provide detailed measurements in the product description rather than relying solely on size labels. We recommend comparing these measurements to a similar item you already own for the best fit. If you're unsure about sizing, please contact us before purchasing.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer eco-friendly gift wrapping for an additional ₹99. Our gift wrapping uses recycled paper and natural jute twine. You can add this option during checkout and include a personalized message for the recipient.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for items in their original condition with tags attached. Due to the unique nature of our products, we handle returns on a case-by-case basis. Please note that customized items and sale items marked as final sale are not eligible for return.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for return.

Can I exchange an item for a different size?

Since our inventory consists of unique vintage items, direct exchanges for different sizes are not always possible. If you need a different size, we recommend returning the item (following our return policy) and placing a new order for the desired item. Our customer service team can help you find similar items in your preferred size.

How long does the refund process take?

Once we receive your returned item and verify its condition, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days. The refund will be issued to the original payment method. Depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

How does buying vintage contribute to sustainability?

Buying vintage and pre-loved items is one of the most sustainable shopping choices you can make. By giving existing items a second life, you're:

  • Reducing demand for new production and associated resource consumption
  • Preventing usable items from ending up in landfills
  • Decreasing your carbon footprint compared to buying new
  • Supporting a circular economy model

Each vintage item you purchase represents a step toward more sustainable consumption habits.

What sustainable practices does Neevstra follow?

Sustainability is at the core of our business model. Beyond selling pre-loved items, we implement sustainable practices throughout our operations:

  • Using eco-friendly cleaning methods for our inventory
  • Packaging orders in recycled and biodegradable materials
  • Partnering with carbon-neutral shipping providers when possible
  • Operating our physical store and warehouse with renewable energy
  • Donating unsold items to local charities rather than discarding them

We're constantly looking for ways to reduce our environmental impact and improve our sustainability practices.
