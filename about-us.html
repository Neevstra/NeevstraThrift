About Neevstra

Sustainable fashion with a vintage soul

Welcome to Neevstra, where sustainability meets style. We are passionate about giving pre-loved clothing a second life while reducing the environmental impact of the fashion industry.

Our Story

Neevstra was founded in 2024 by a group of fashion enthusiasts who were concerned about the growing waste in the fashion industry. We started as a small thrift store in Hyderabad, Telangana and have since grown into an online platform connecting conscious shoppers with unique, sustainable fashion finds.

Our mission is simple: to promote sustainable fashion choices by curating high-quality second-hand clothing that doesn't compromise on style or quality. We believe that fashion should be both beautiful and responsible.

Our Values

Sustainability

We are committed to reducing fashion waste by extending the lifecycle of clothing through thrifting and upcycling.

Quality

We carefully select each item in our collection, ensuring that only the highest quality pieces make it to our store.

Community

We believe in building a community of like-minded individuals who value sustainable fashion and ethical consumption.

Our Process

Every item in our collection goes through a rigorous selection and quality control process:

  1. Sourcing: We source our items from various channels, including individual donors, estate sales, and vintage collectors.
  2. Selection: Our fashion experts carefully select items based on quality, condition, style, and uniqueness.
  3. Cleaning & Restoration: Each item is professionally cleaned and, if necessary, restored to ensure it's in excellent condition.
  4. Photography & Listing: We photograph each item and create detailed descriptions to help you make informed choices.
  5. Sustainable Packaging: When you make a purchase, we ship your items in eco-friendly packaging.
