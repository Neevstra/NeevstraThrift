About Us

Our Story

Neevstra was born from a passion for sustainable fashion and a desire to give pre-loved items a second life. Founded in 2025, our curated thrift store focuses on quality, uniqueness, and environmental responsibility.

Our Mission

We believe in reducing waste and promoting conscious consumption. Each item in our collection is carefully selected for its quality, style, and character - giving you access to sustainable fashion without compromising on aesthetics.

Our Process

Every item at Neevstra goes through a meticulous selection, cleaning, and quality check process. We ensure that each piece meets our standards of excellence before it reaches our shelves and eventually, your wardrobe.
