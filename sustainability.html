The Fashion Industry's Environmental Impact

The fashion industry is one of the largest polluters in the world, responsible for:

  • 10% of global carbon emissions
  • 20% of global wastewater
  • 85% of textiles ending up in landfills each year
  • Microplastic pollution from synthetic fabrics

By choosing second-hand clothing, you're helping to reduce this impact by extending the lifecycle of garments and reducing the demand for new production.

Our Environmental Impact

Water Conservation

By selling pre-loved clothing, we help save the thousands of gallons of water that would be used to produce new garments.

Carbon Reduction

Second-hand clothing has a significantly lower carbon footprint compared to new items, helping to combat climate change.

Waste Reduction

We're keeping clothing out of landfills by giving them a second life in someone else's wardrobe.

Our Sustainability Commitments

Eco-Friendly Packaging

All our orders are shipped in recycled or biodegradable packaging materials. We've eliminated plastic from our shipping process and use paper tape and recycled cardboard boxes.

Carbon-Neutral Shipping

We offset the carbon emissions from all our shipments by investing in verified carbon offset projects focused on renewable energy and reforestation.

Clothing Donations

Items that don't meet our quality standards for resale are donated to local charities or recycled through textile recycling programs, ensuring nothing goes to waste.

Tree Planting Initiative

For every 10 orders placed, we plant a tree through our partnership with reforestation organizations, helping to offset carbon and restore ecosystems.

Join Our Sustainable Fashion Movement

By shopping at Neevstra, you're not just finding unique fashion pieces—you're making a positive impact on the planet. Together, we can transform the fashion industry into a more sustainable and responsible one.
